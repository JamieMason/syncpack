---
title: DiffersToHighestOrLowestSemver
status: fixable
description: Dependency version differs from the highest or lowest semver version in its group
---

## When this happens

- ✓ Instance is in a [Highest Semver](VERSION_GROUP_HIGHEST_SEMVER) or [Lowest Semver](VERSION_GROUP_LOWEST_SEMVER) version group
- ✘ Instance mismatches highest/lowest semver in its group

## Severity

<SeverityTable fixEffect="Rewrite the instance to match the highest/lowest semver in its group.">

Configurable via [`severity`](REF_SEVERITY) on a [Highest Semver](VERSION_GROUP_HIGHEST_SEMVER) or [Lowest Semver](VERSION_GROUP_LOWEST_SEMVER) version group.

</SeverityTable>
